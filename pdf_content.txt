1. Document Purpose
This master document defines the full website architecture for Novelle before visual design and development begin.
It combines brand understanding, sitemap decisions, page-by-page section planning, content direction, copywriting,
UI guidance, SEO structure, forms, technical notes and future scalability.
The copy included in this document is polished working copy. It is realistic enough to use in a first website draft, but
it should be treated as flexible placeholder content until Novelle's team confirms exact courses, treatment names,
doctor/faculty details, legal disclaimers, clinic timings, location and admissions process.
The goal is to align the client, designer, developer and content team around one clear blueprint so the website feels
premium, clinically credible and conversion-focused from the first build.
Core output: a luxury medical-aesthetic website that positions Novelle as an authoritative institution for aesthetic
and laser education in Dubai and the GCC, while also supporting lead generation for aesthetic consultations and
treatments.

2. Brand Understanding
Novelle is a premium aesthetic and laser education institution with a dual purpose: to train elite practitioners and to
provide discerning clients with safety-led aesthetic treatments. The website must balance two personalities at the
same time: the precision of a medical academy and the refinement of a luxury beauty destination.
The brand should never feel like a regular salon, a generic training center or a low-cost course website. The digital
experience should feel calm, high-end, structured, trustworthy and aspirational.
 Innovating Beauty Through Education
Brand promise
Novelle is the definitive destination where scientific mastery leads to aesthetic excellence. The website should
communicate that every course, treatment and consultation is shaped by clinical understanding, ethical practice,
modern technology and refined results.

7. Typography & Layout System
Brand typography should use Alice for elegant, premium titles and Poppins for clean, readable body content. The
website should keep heading lines spacious and avoid compressed blocks of text.

Spacing and layout
●
Use large vertical spacing between sections, especially on desktop.
●
Keep maximum text width controlled. Premium pages should not have long full-width paragraphs.
●
Use 2-column layouts for academy/treatment dual sections, curriculum cards and benefit blocks.
●
Use soft rounded cards, thin gold borders and minimal shadows.
●
Use charcoal sections sparingly for high-impact moments, such as final CTA and footer.
Imagery style
●
Clean clinical photography with warm luxury grading.
●
Close-up aesthetic equipment visuals, laser treatment rooms, skin texture, academy training setups and faculty-led sessions.
●
Avoid stock images that look like generic salons or beauty parlours.
●
Use gold light leaks, soft gradients and subtle glow around premium hero imagery.
●
Before-after images must include ethical disclaimers and should not overpromise results.
